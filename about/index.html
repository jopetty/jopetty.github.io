About

I’m an undergraduate in Saybrook College at Yale University studying mathematics and linguistics. My research interests lie in theoretical syntax, computational semantics, language modeling, and semitic languages. I currently work in CLAY Lab to investigate the acquisition of semantic representations of anaphora by neural networks.
