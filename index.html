 Free and Open Source Projects 

Huruf (حروف)

Huruf is a Chrome Extension that makes Arabic, Farsi, and many other languages easier to read online by replacing small, obfuscating fonts with larger and easy-to-read styles, making these languages more accessible to everyone.

Bitwisr

Building upon the national push to bring computer science education to students, I am developing a free and interdisciplinary CS curriculum, combining resources and sample code on GitHub with fun overviews on YouTube with the goal of making CS education free and accesible to everyone.

Punnett2

Punnett2 is a web app which generates Punnett squares for given genotypic inputs. I built it during the unit on Mendelian Inheritance in my AP Biology class.

Code/on

Also made for AP Biology, Code/on translates RNA codons and anti-codons into a sequence of their corresponing Amino Acids, which makes looking up polypeptide sequences a breeze.
